Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Sudoku ("we", "us", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Sudoku is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, and we do not transmit any of your data to external services under our control.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device to provide a seamless experience:

  • Game progress — your current puzzle state, so you can resume where you left off.
  • Statistics — games played, win rate, best times, and streaks.
  • Settings — your preferences such as theme, sound, and haptic feedback toggles.

This data is stored using Apple's standard on-device storage and is never sent to us.

Third-Party Services

Apple Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple may process your data according to their own privacy policy. Game Center is used for:

  • Leaderboards — your best completion times are submitted to Game Center leaderboards.
  • Achievements — your in-game accomplishments are reported to Game Center.

Game Center sign-in is optional. The app works fully without it. For details on how Apple handles your Game Center data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

iCloud

If you enable iCloud sync in the app's settings, your statistics and preferences may be synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy. You can disable this at any time in the app's settings.

Analytics and Advertising

We do not use any analytics frameworks, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs. The app contains no ads.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. No account creation is required, and no personal data is collected.

Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. You can delete all data at any time by deleting the app. If you use iCloud sync, you can manage your iCloud data through your device's Settings → Apple ID → iCloud.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page.
